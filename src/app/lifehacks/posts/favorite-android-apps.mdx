## Communication

### Signal

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.thoughtcrime.securesms)

**Why I love it:** End-to-end encrypted messaging that actually respects privacy. Clean interface, disappearing messages, and doesn't harvest your data like other messaging apps.

### Slack

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Slack)

**Why I love it:** Essential for work communication. The mobile app is well-designed for quick responses and staying updated without being chained to my desk.

## Privacy & Security

### Bitwarden

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.x8bit.bitwarden)

**Why I love it:** Open-source password manager with excellent mobile app. Auto-fill works seamlessly, and the free tier is generous. Self-hosting option is a plus for security professionals.

### ProtonVPN

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ch.protonvpn.android)

**Why I love it:** Privacy-focused VPN with a solid free tier. Swiss privacy laws, no-logs policy, and secure core servers. Essential when using public Wi-Fi.

## Reading & Learning

### Pocket

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ideashower.readitlater.pro)

**Why I love it:** Save articles to read later without distractions. Clean reading view, offline access, and tagging system help me organize content for research.

### Kindle

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.amazon.kindle)

**Why I love it:** Massive library of technical books and security publications. Highlights sync across devices, making it easy to reference important passages later.

### Moon Reader

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.flyersoft.moonreader)

**Why I love it:** Feature-rich e-book reader with support for EPUB, PDF, and many other formats. Highly customizable reading experience with themes, fonts, and page-turn effects.

## Utilities

### Google Keep

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.keep)

**Why I love it:** Quick capture for random thoughts, shopping lists, and voice notes. The widget on home screen makes jotting down ideas effortless.

## Health & Fitness

### Garmin Connect

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.garmin.android.apps.connectmobile)

**Why I love it:** Comprehensive fitness and health tracking paired with my Garmin watch. Detailed activity metrics, sleep tracking, and training insights all in one place.

## The Core Principle

I'm very selective about apps I install. Each app must:
1. Solve a real problem in my life
2. Respect privacy (open-source preferred)
3. Work reliably without constant maintenance
4. Justify the storage space and permissions it needs

Quality over quantity. My phone is a tool, not a distraction machine.
